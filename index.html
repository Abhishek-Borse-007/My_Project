How EMI is calculated

The formula behind your monthly payment.

EMI depends on the loan amount, monthly interest rate, and total number of months in your loan tenure. Higher rates and longer tenure increase the total interest paid, even if the monthly EMI looks manageable.

Try the calculator with your numbers

EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)n / ((1 + r)n - 1)

  • P = loan amount
  • r = monthly interest rate
  • n = total monthly installments
